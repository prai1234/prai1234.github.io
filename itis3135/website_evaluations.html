Website Evaluation Examples

ExampleSite.com

ExampleSite.com is an e-commerce platform specializing in handmade crafts.

  • Contrast: Utilizes a dark blue header with white text, enhancing readability.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts and color palette reinforces brand identity.
  • Alignment: Elements are aligned in a grid layout, providing a clean appearance.
  • Proximity: Related products are grouped together, enhancing user experience.

ClutteredSite.com

ClutteredSite.com is a personal blog covering various topics.

  • Contrast: Light gray text on a white background reduces readability.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent fonts and color schemes lead to a disjointed feel.
  • Alignment: Text and images are scattered without alignment, creating a chaotic look.
  • Proximity: Unrelated content is placed next to each other, confusing the user.
