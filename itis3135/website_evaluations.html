Website Evaluations

YouTube - Good Website

A popular video-sharing platform known for its user-friendly interface and engaging content.

  • Contrast: Uses contrasting colors effectively to highlight important elements.
  • Repetition: Consistent layout and design elements across different pages and sections.
  • Alignment: All elements are well-aligned, creating a neat and organized appearance.
  • Proximity: Groups related content closely together, enhancing usability and navigation.

Assessment: YouTube excels in user experience with its clear design principles and intuitive navigation.

Craigslist - Bad Website

A classified advertisements website that focuses on simplicity but lacks modern design principles.

  • Contrast: Limited contrast makes it challenging to differentiate sections and elements.
  • Repetition: Basic design elements are repeated but lack visual consistency across the site.
  • Alignment: Elements are often misaligned, leading to a cluttered and disorganized appearance.
  • Proximity: Unrelated content is placed closely together, causing confusion in navigation.

Assessment: Craigslist struggles with modern design principles, resulting in a less user-friendly experience.
