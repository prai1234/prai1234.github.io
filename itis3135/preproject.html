Project Name

ManojKRai.com

Project Overview

This website will serve to promote Manoj K. Rai’s music, activism, and the work of the Love & Support Foundation. It will inform users about his journey as a musician, community leader, and social activist. The website will also highlight his work with underprivileged youth and other initiatives supported by the foundation.

Intended Users

The intended users of this website are fans of Manoj K. Rai, people interested in his community initiatives, and anyone wanting to learn more about his music and the Love & Support Foundation.

Overview of Website Content

  • Home Page: Introduction to Manoj K. Rai, his music, and community work.
  • Gallery Page: Collection of images from events, concerts, and community activities.
  • Albums Page: Display of Manoj’s music albums, with audio samples and download links.
  • Videos Page: Embedded music videos, interviews, and community project videos.
  • Contact Page: Contact form for inquiries and social media links.

Wireframe

Sitemap

1. Home Page

Page Name: Home Page

Purpose: Introduce the site’s purpose, Manoj K. Rai’s work, and the Love & Support Foundation.

Audience: Fans of Manoj K. Rai, visitors interested in music, and social activism.

Type of Content: Introduction to Manoj K. Rai's music career, information about the Love & Support Foundation, and key initiatives and community projects.

Dynamic Functionality: Navigation to other pages (Gallery, Albums, Videos, Contact). A welcome message or slider showcasing images and key content.

Data Fields: None for this page.

Validation: No data validation required.

Notes: A prominent section for introducing the artist’s social causes and music. A slideshow of key events or albums.

2. Gallery Page

Page Name: Gallery Page

Purpose: Showcase images from concerts, events, and community activities.

Audience: Fans, potential event sponsors, and supporters of the Love & Support Foundation.

Type of Content: A collection of photos in a grid layout, highlighted images from concerts, performances, and community outreach programs.

Dynamic Functionality: Image carousel/slideshow to browse through high-quality images. Zoom-in functionality for detailed viewing of each image.

Data Fields: None.

Validation: No data validation required.

Notes: Display images with text captions for context (concert date, location, cause supported). Use a lightbox effect to enlarge images on click.

3. Albums Page

Page Name: Albums Page

Purpose: Display Manoj K. Rai’s music albums with audio samples and download links.

Audience: Music fans, potential buyers, and streaming platforms.

Type of Content: Album covers with tracklist, audio samples from each album for preview, and links to purchase or stream the album.

Dynamic Functionality: Audio player for each album sample. Links to streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, etc.

Data Fields: None for the album display itself, but each album may have a link to a purchase platform.

Validation: Validation required for any user-submitted feedback (if applicable).

Notes: Clear call-to-action buttons for streaming and purchasing albums. Highlight any available music videos from albums.

4. Videos Page

Page Name: Videos Page

Purpose: Showcase Manoj K. Rai’s music videos, interviews, and community project videos.

Audience: Fans of Manoj K. Rai, general visitors interested in videos, and supporters of social causes.

Type of Content: Embedded music videos, video clips from interviews, charity events, and community work.

Dynamic Functionality: Video player for music videos and interviews. Option to view full-screen or in embedded view. Like, comment, or share features (optional).

Data Fields: None for video content.

Validation: No validation required unless comments or feedback are collected.

Notes: Ensure videos are categorized (e.g., music videos, interviews, community projects). Include captions or subtitles for accessibility.

5. Contact Page

Page Name: Contact Page

Purpose: Provide a way for visitors to get in touch with Manoj K. Rai or the Love & Support Foundation for inquiries, collaborations, or support.

Audience: Fans, media, event organizers, and potential collaborators.

Type of Content: Contact form for inquiries, links to social media accounts, foundation address, and phone number.

Dynamic Functionality: Contact form that includes fields for name, email, message, and possibly a subject dropdown. Form submission with an acknowledgment message.

Data Fields: Name, Email, Subject, Message.

Validation: Name and email validation to ensure correct formats. Message field validation to ensure it’s not left blank.

Notes: Include a clear "Thank you" message after form submission. Consider using CAPTCHA or other methods to prevent spam.
