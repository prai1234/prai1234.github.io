Project Name

ManojKRai.com

Project Overview

This website will serve to promote Manoj K. Rai’s music, activism, and the work of the Love & Support Foundation. It will inform users about his journey as a musician, community leader, and social activist. The website will also highlight his work with underprivileged youth and other initiatives supported by the foundation.

Intended Users

The intended users of this website are fans of Manoj K. Rai, people interested in his community initiatives, and anyone wanting to learn more about his music and the Love & Support Foundation.

Overview of Website Content

  • Home Page: Introduction to Manoj K. Rai, his music, and community work.
  • Gallery Page: Collection of images from events, concerts, and community activities.
  • Albums Page: Display of Manoj’s music albums, with audio samples and download links.
  • Videos Page: Embedded music videos, interviews, and community project videos.
  • Contact Page: Contact form for inquiries and social media links.
